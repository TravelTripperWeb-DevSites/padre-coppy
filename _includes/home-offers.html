Activities & Events

Unique discoveries

Giddy up! Between monthly wine tastings, signature whiskey events and local beer dinners, we’re keeping your social calendar so full you’ll never have to leave the hotel! But that’s not to say there isn’t fun to be found outside our four walls. Enjoy an evening entertainment with our neighbor the historic Fox Theater. Or go boating and fishing on beautiful Lake Webb and discover a fascinating 18th-century village at the Kern County Museum. However you choose to pair your stay, you’re in good company with the Padre.
