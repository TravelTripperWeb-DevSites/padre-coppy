PETS

The Padre Hotel is a pet friendly hotel. We charge a $50 non-refundable pet cleaning fee per pet. Occasionally the Hotel will need to contact pet owners if the pet is left unattended and causing a disturbance. Please leave your cell phone contact information with the front desk for this purpose. If the pet causes any damage to the room or furniture, you will incur further fees.
